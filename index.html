I am an Applied Scientist at Amazon AGI Foundations, working on multi-modal and reasoning foundational models (Amazon Nova). I received my PhD in Computer Science from Columbia University, advised by Shih-Fu Chang. Prior to that, I obtained my B.S. in Electrical Engineering and B.S. in Applied Mathematics at UC San Diego.
